# BetterPrompts - Image Generation Prompt

## Project: AI-Powered Prompt Enhancement Platform

An intelligent platform that transforms basic user inputs into professionally optimized prompts using 12 advanced engineering techniques.

---

## Image Generation Prompt

**Scene Description:**
A transformative visual metaphor showing the journey from simple to sophisticated. On the left side, a basic, rough-hewn text block floats in a misty void—plain white text on gray, representing a raw user prompt. As it travels through a magnificent crystalline prism at the center, it explodes into a spectacular array of refined, golden light beams on the right side. These beams form into elegant, structured prompt text that glows with an ethereal quality. The prism itself is a stunning piece of technological art—part diamond, part circuit board, with 12 distinct facets representing each optimization technique.

**Composition:**

- Left third: "Before" state—simple text block, muted colors, unrefined
- Center: The transformation engine—a floating crystalline AI prism with visible internal light refraction
- Right third: "After" state—beautiful, structured, glowing optimized prompt
- Background: Gradient from dark uncertainty (left) to bright clarity (right)
- Foreground: Subtle data particles flowing toward the prism
- Above the prism: Small icons representing the 12 techniques orbiting like electrons

**Color Palette:**

- Left side: Muted grays (#6B7280), dull whites (#E5E7EB)
- Center prism: Crystal clear with rainbow refractions, electric purple core (#8B5CF6)
- Right side: Warm gold (#F59E0B), brilliant white (#FFFFFF), soft amber glow
- Background gradient: Slate blue (#1E293B) to soft lavender (#C4B5FD) to warm cream (#FEF3C7)
- Accent: Vibrant teal (#14B8A6) for interactive elements

**Lighting:**

- The prism is the primary light source, emanating transformative energy
- Soft ambient light from the right side (representing enlightenment)
- Rim lighting on the text blocks to define their edges
- God rays emanating from the prism's output side
- Subtle lens flare on the brightest points

**Mood/Atmosphere:**
Magical yet professional. The image should feel like witnessing alchemy—the transformation of something ordinary into something extraordinary. Clean, modern SaaS aesthetic meets mystical transformation. Conveys accessibility (anyone can use this) combined with sophistication (powered by advanced AI).

**Key Visual Elements:**

- The 12-faceted prism with each facet subtly labeled (Chain of Thought, Tree of Thoughts, Few-Shot, etc.)
- Before/after text contrast (messy → structured)
- Speed lines suggesting fast processing (<200ms)
- Small "40%" improvement indicator floating near the output
- Neural network patterns visible inside the prism
- DeBERTa-inspired transformer architecture subtly embedded in the prism's core

**Style References:**

- Apple product photography (clean, minimal, premium)
- Stripe/Linear design language (modern SaaS)
- Disney magic transformation sequences (the moment of change)
- Notion and Figma marketing visuals (friendly tech)

**Technical Specifications:**

- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3 (versatile for landing pages)
- Style: 3D render with stylized realism
- Render quality: Clean, crisp edges, professional marketing quality
- Depth of field: Medium, keeping both input and output in focus with slight bokeh on background

**Text Overlay Space:**
Reserve top-center area for headline: "Transform Any Prompt Into Perfection" and bottom area for CTA button space.
